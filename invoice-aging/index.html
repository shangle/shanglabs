Invoice Aging Tracker Help

What Is This Tool?

The Invoice Aging Tracker categorizes outstanding invoices by how long they've been overdue. Upload your invoice data to see a summary by aging bucket, identify high-risk overdue accounts, and generate collection reports.

Getting Started

  1. Prepare Your CSV -- Your file should include columns for Invoice #, Amount, Due Date, and Customer Name.
  2. Upload the File -- Drag and drop your CSV onto the upload area or click to browse.
  3. Review Aging Summary -- See total outstanding amounts broken down by aging period.
  4. Export Report -- Download the aging report for your records or collection team.

Aging Buckets

Current: 0-30 days
31-60 Days: Mildly overdue
61-90 Days: Moderately overdue
90+ Days: Seriously overdue

CSV File Format

Your CSV should contain these columns (headers required):

  • Invoice #: Unique invoice identifier
  • Amount: Dollar amount owed (numeric, no currency symbol needed)
  • Due Date: When payment was/is due (YYYY-MM-DD or MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Customer Name: Name of the customer/company
Tip: Focus collection efforts on the 61-90 and 90+ day buckets first. These accounts have the highest risk of becoming uncollectible, and early intervention improves recovery rates.

Best Practices

  • Review aging reports weekly to catch overdue invoices early.
  • Automate reminder emails for invoices entering the 31-60 day bucket.
  • Consider offering early payment discounts to reduce aging.
  • Escalate 90+ day accounts to a collections specialist.
Note: All data stays in your browser. No invoice data is transmitted to any server.
