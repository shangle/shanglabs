# Cash Flow Forecaster

A client-side only rolling cash projection tool for business owners and finance teams.

## Features

✅ **13-Week Rolling Forecast** - projects your cash position across 13 weeks
✅ **Recurring Cash Flows** - add weekly or monthly income/expenses
✅ **One-Time Flows** - track ad-hoc inflows and outflows
✅ **Low Cash Alerts** - weeks below minimum threshold highlighted in red
✅ **Burn Rate & Runway** - automatic calculation of burn rate and weeks until cash runs out
✅ **Visual Chart** - line chart showing cash position trend over time
✅ **CSV Export** - download your forecast for analysis or sharing

## How to Use

1. **Set Your Parameters**
   - Starting cash balance (what you have now)
   - Minimum threshold (warning level for low cash)
   - Start date for the forecast

2. **Add Recurring Flows**
   - Weekly or monthly income (revenue, subscriptions, etc.)
   - Weekly or monthly expenses (rent, payroll, utilities, etc.)
   - Use negative numbers for outflows, positive for inflows

3. **Add One-Time Flows**
   - Ad-hoc expenses (equipment purchases, tax payments, etc.)
   - One-time income (investments, grants, etc.)
   - Specify which week the flow occurs (0-13)

4. **Generate & Analyze**
   - Click "Generate 13-Week Forecast"
   - Review metrics: burn rate, runway, ending balance
   - Check the chart for visual trend
   - Identify weeks highlighted in red (below threshold)

5. **Export**
   - Click "Export to CSV" to download your forecast
   - Use in Excel, Google Sheets, or share with team

## Tech Stack

- **Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript** - no frameworks, no server required
- **Chart.js** - lightweight charting library (via CDN)
- **100% Client-Side** - data never leaves your browser
