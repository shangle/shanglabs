The Problem with ACH Files

NACHA files are deceptively simple: 94-character records with strict positional rules. A single misplaced character causes rejection from the Federal Reserve. Traditional tools are expensive, require software installations, and force you to send financial data to third-party servers.

↓

The Shanglabs Difference

100% browser-based validation with zero data transmission. Every character of your ACH file stays on your device. Open a file, see validation results instantly, export reports, and close the browser — no installation, no subscription, no data leaving your control.
