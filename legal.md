## Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

### Overview

At Shanglabs, we take your privacy seriously. All of our tools operate 100% client-side, meaning your data never leaves your browser unless you choose to download it.

**What we collect:**

**What we don't collect:**
- Personal information
- Usage statistics
- Browser fingerprints
- IP addresses
- File contents you process

### Client-Side Processing

All data processing happens entirely in your web browser. This includes:
- NACHA file parsing and validation
- Financial calculations and analysis
- QR code generation
- Any file uploads or content you work with

### Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:
- **Chart.js** (CDN) - For rendering charts in financial tools
- **QRCode.js** (CDN) - For generating QR codes
- **Static hosting** - GitHub Pages for serving our website

These services do not collect your data.

### Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. We use only essential session cookies for:
- Maintaining your preferences (e.g., theme selection)
- These are stored locally in your browser

- **Right to access:** You can view all data you've entered by checking your browser's local storage
- **Right to deletion:** Clear your browser data to remove all stored information
- **Right to export:** Download your data at any time using our built-in export features

### Data Security

- All data stays on your device
- We use HTTPS for all communications
- No data is transmitted to our servers
- No third-party analytics or tracking

### Children's Privacy

Our tools are not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

### International Users

Shanglabs operates globally. Your data is processed locally in your browser, regardless of your location.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we evolve. Any changes will be posted here with a revised date.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us through https://shangle.me
