## Pricing Formula Validation

### Original Formula: Download Price = Dev Cost / 100

If we used AI cost only:
- NACHA Viewer: $0.72 / 100 = **$0.007** (too low for sustainability)

### Revised Formula (Hybrid):
**Download Price = (Human Time × $20/hr + AI Cost) / 100**

Rationale:
- Human time remains valuable (review, testing, decisions)
- $20/hr acknowledges AI acceleration but rewards skill
- $0.72 AI cost for NACHA Viewer
- 8 hours human time × $20/hr = $160
- Total: $160.72 / 100 = **$1.61**

**Current listing:** $1.30
**Validation:** ✅ Undercut by 19% (fair premium)

## Lessons Learned

### What Works Well
1. **Iterative development:** Small AI prompts, frequent commits
2. **Quality over quantity:** One good AI response > 3 mediocre ones
3. **Human review essential:** AI writes code, humans ensure quality

### What's Challenging
1. **Context limits:** Large apps require multiple sessions
2. **Model hallucination:** Requires careful verification
3. **Edge cases:** AI often misses, human catches them

### What's Surprising
1. **Cost is negligible:** Even complex apps cost < $1 in AI processing
2. **Speed is remarkable:** What takes human weeks takes AI hours
3. **Quality is competitive:** AI code produces professional results
